About us

WJR multi projects is 100% Black Owned and was founded in 2017 by Mr Mzilikazi Wilford Baloi as sole Director. The head office is based in Gauteng Pretoria, and we are planning to expand to have footprint across all the provinces of the country.

WJR is an engineering company specialising in Electrical and Mechanical engineering for both Industrial; Commercial and Domestic infrastructures. Our resources allow us to execute and complete projects within budget and time viz: Installation of HVAC, Electrical panels, Installation and Commisioning of garage and gate motors, installation of solar panels, Air-condissioning, all mechanical works and Civil works.
